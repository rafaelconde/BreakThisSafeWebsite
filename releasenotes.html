How to play:

  • Each game has 3 rounds;
  • Guess correctly all three to win;
  • One wrong answer and you're out;
  • Rotate the Digital Crown to scrub the knob;
  • When you start feeling some haptic feedback, that means you're close to the answer;
  • One of the vibrations is going to be stronger than the others, that's the right answer;
  • Tap the round button in the middle to select your answer;
